Opening Procedures

Shop Procedure

Morning Routine:

  1. Unlock doors and disarm security system
  2. Turn on all necessary lights and equipment
  3. Prepare espresso machine (fill water, warm up)
  4. Check inventory and restock as needed
  5. Set up pastry display case
  6. Prepare coffee brewers and tea stations
  7. Count register float and prepare till
  8. Wipe down all surfaces and ensure cleanliness
  9. Put out sidewalk sign and open curtains
  10. Turn on music system at appropriate volume

Closing Procedures

Shop Procedure

Evening Routine:

  1. Begin closing procedures 30 minutes before actual closing
  2. Start shutting down equipment not in use
  3. Clean espresso machine and steam wands
  4. Dispose of any expired food items
  5. Package remaining pastries for next day or donation
  6. Wipe down all counters, tables, and surfaces
  7. Sweep and mop floors
  8. Take out trash and recycling
  9. Count register and prepare deposit
  10. Set alarm system and lock all doors
  11. Complete closing checklist and sign off

Hazelnut

Drink

Temperature:

Milk Type:

Hot Procedure (Regular Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and stir
  3. Steam 180ml of regular milk to 65°C (150°F) with microfoam
  4. Pour milk into espresso in a circular motion
  5. Hold back foam with spoon for first 3/4 of pour
  6. Add remaining foam on top
  7. Optional: dust with cinnamon or cocoa powder

Cold Procedure (Regular Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and stir
  3. Add 180ml cold regular milk (no frothing)
  4. Stir gently to combine
  5. Add ice cubes to fill glass
  6. Optional: top with whipped cream

Frappe Procedure (Regular Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml regular milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth and frothy
  5. Pour into serving glass
  6. Top with whipped cream and hazelnut drizzle

Hot Procedure (Oat Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and stir
  3. Steam 180ml of oat milk to 60°C (140°F) - creates less foam
  4. Pour milk into espresso gently
  5. Oat milk may not create latte art as easily
  6. Optional: sprinkle with cinnamon

Cold Procedure (Oat Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and stir
  3. Add 180ml cold oat milk (no frothing)
  4. Stir gently to combine
  5. Add ice cubes to fill glass
  6. Optional: top with non-dairy whipped cream

Frappe Procedure (Oat Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml oat milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Pour into serving glass
  6. Top with non-dairy whipped cream

Hot Procedure (Oat Choco):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and 15ml chocolate syrup
  3. Steam 180ml of oat milk to 60°C (140°F)
  4. Pour milk into espresso and syrup mixture
  5. Stir gently to combine chocolate
  6. Optional: dust with cocoa powder

Cold Procedure (Oat Choco):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and 15ml chocolate syrup
  3. Add 180ml cold oat milk (no frothing)
  4. Stir vigorously to combine chocolate
  5. Add ice cubes to fill glass
  6. Optional: chocolate drizzle on top

Frappe Procedure (Oat Choco):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso (30ml)
  2. Add 20ml hazelnut syrup and 15ml chocolate syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml oat milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Pour into serving glass
  6. Top with chocolate drizzle and cocoa powder

Dark Mocha Frappe

Drink

Milk Type:

Procedure (Regular Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso
  2. Add 30ml dark chocolate syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml regular milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Top with whipped cream and chocolate drizzle
  6. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings before serving

Procedure (Oat Milk):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso
  2. Add 30ml dark chocolate syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml oat milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Top with non-dairy whipped cream and chocolate drizzle
  6. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings before serving

Procedure (Oat Choco):

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso
  2. Add 25ml dark chocolate syrup and 15ml chocolate syrup
  3. Combine with 150ml oat milk and 2 cups of ice
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Top with non-dairy whipped cream and extra chocolate drizzle
  6. Garnish with chocolate shavings and cocoa powder

Blueberry Muffin

Muffin

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F)
  2. Mix dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, salt
  3. In separate bowl, mix wet ingredients: milk, egg, melted butter
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, fold in blueberries
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
  7. Cool on wire rack before serving

Nachos

Snacks

How to Prepare Nachos

  • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • Arrange tortilla chips evenly on a serving plate or baking tray.
  • Sprinkle shredded cheese generously over the chips.
  • Add optional toppings like jalapeños, olives, or diced tomatoes.
  • Heat in oven for about 5 minutes or until cheese melts.
  • Drizzle with cheese sauce or sour cream (optional).
  • Serve warm and enjoy!
